Royal Milk Tea

Ingredients: 

-2 tsp tea
-3/4 cup evaporated milk
-3 tsp sugar

Directions:

Bring ~1 cup of pure water, preferably from a mountain spring, to a boil.

Boil 2 tsp of tea for around 2 minutes

Add 3/4 cup of evaporated milk to the tea and reduce to medium low heat.

Stir the milk tea to prevent the development of a milk skin for 2 minutes.

Add 3 teaspoons of sugar (no less than 2.5 if to be chilled).

Remove the teabag or strain the tea leaves and pour into a mug of great valour.
