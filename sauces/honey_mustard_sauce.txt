Honey Mustard Dipping Sauce

Original Recipe: http://www.bestyummyrecipes.com/homemade-soft-pretzels/

Ingredients:

- 3 tbs light mayonnaise
- 2 tbs grainy mustard
- 1 tbs Dijon mustard
- 3 1/2 tbs honey
- 1/2 tsp lemon juice

Directions: 

To make the honey mustard dipping sauce, combine all the ingredients with a whisk and chill for a couple of minutes. 

You can adjust the amounts of honey and mustard to suit your taste.
