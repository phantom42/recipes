Banana Bread

Original Recipe: The Interwebs
Recipe modified by: https://github.com/phantom42

Ingredients:

4 Bananas
1/3 c melted butter
1 c sugar
1 egg beaten
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 c flour

Directions:

Mix butter and bananas.

Add sugar, egg, vanilla. Mix.

Add baking soda, salt. Mix.

Add flour. Mix.

Bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes.
