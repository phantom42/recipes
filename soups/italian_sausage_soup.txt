Italian Sausage Soup

Original Recipe: http://community.livejournal.com/cooking/7007682.html

Ingredients:

-1 lb hot italian sausage
-1 shallot (2 if they are small or half an onion)
-2 cloves garlic
-1 tbsp butter
-1 can dice tomatoes swith basil oregano and garlic
-1 can white beans
-2 cups chopped fresh baby spinach
-1/2-3/4 cup small tube pasta (ziti/penne)
-28 oz beef stock (low salt)

Directions:

Remove sausage from casing. 

Crumble and browne them up in the stock pot (or dutch oven if you have one )

Remove meat, leaving renderings.

In the sausage renderings with butter, sautee the shallots and garlic.

Add beef stock. Bring to a simmer.

Added the tomatoes beans, and meat back in as well as the pasta, cover and let it simmer until the shells are done.

Remove from the heat stir in the spinach replace the lid for 5 min.
