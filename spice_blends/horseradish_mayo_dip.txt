Horseradish Mayo Dip

Original Recipe: http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/food-network-kitchens/almost-famous-fried-pickles-recipe.html

Ingredients:

- 1/4 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon drained horseradish
- 2 teaspoons ketchup
- 1/4 teaspoon Cajun seasoning

Directions:

Mix the mayonnaise, horseradish, ketchup and Cajun seasoning in a bowl; set aside.
