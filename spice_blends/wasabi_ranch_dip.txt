Wasabi Ranch Dip

Original Recipe: http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/food-network-kitchens/almost-famous-green-bean-fries-recipe.html

Ingredients:

- 1/2 cup ranch dressing
- 1/2 cup peeled, seeded,chopped cucumber
- 1 tablespoon horseradish, drained
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 1 teaspoon distilled white vinegar
- 2 teaspoons wasabi powder (found in the spice aisle or with the Asian foods)
- Pinch of cayenne pepper
- Kosher salt

Directions:

Puree the dressing, cucumber, horseradish, milk, vinegar, wasabi powder, cayenne pepper, and salt to taste in a blender until smooth. 

Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
