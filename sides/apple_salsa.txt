Apple Salsa

Original Recipe: http://www.epicurious.com/recipes/food/views/Black-Beans-and-Rice-with-Chicken-and-Apple-Salsa-378344

Ingredients:

 - 1 cup chopped peeled Granny Smith apple
 - 1/2 cup chopped cilantro, divided
 - 1/3 cup finely chopped red onion, divided
 - 1 teaspoon (or more) fresh lime juice

Directions:

Combine apple, 1/4 cup cilantro, 2 tablespoons onion, and 1 teaspoon lime juice in a small bowl; toss to coat.
