Basic Meatballs

Ingredients:

-1 lb ground meat (sausage out of casings, 80/20 beef, chorizo or combination)
-1/4 cup panko
-1/4 cup parmesan cheese
-4 cloves minced garlic
-1 tsp italian seasoning
-1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
-1 egg (beaten) + 1/2 tbsp milk

Directions:

Mix all ingredients in a bowl.

Roll into 1 inch balls.

Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until outside is browned and insides are no longer pink.
